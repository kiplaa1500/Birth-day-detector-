Day Detector And its respective AKAN Name.

Birth day detector is a simple app which help you to identify the exact day you were born through input of;

  • CC : century
  • YY : year
  • MM : Month
  • DD : day

Akan names is generaly based in Ghana adopted to be used in naming children according to day when he or she is born.
