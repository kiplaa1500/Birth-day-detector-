Birth Day Detector And its respective AKAN Name.

Akan names is generaly based in Ghana adopted to be used in naming children according to day when he or she is born.

Male Names.

  1. Sunday : Kwasi
  2. Monday : Kwadwo
  3. Tuesday : Kwabena
  4. Wednesday : Kwaku
  5. Thursday : Yaw
  6. Friday : Kofi
  7. Saturday : Kwame
